About Conor

Based In Ireland, I record for many artists and tour throughout Europe, US and Canada.

  • Foy Vance, 2012 to present
  • Gretchen Peters, 2015 to present.
  • Other singer songwriters include Ryan McMullan, Duke Special, Ben Glover, The Darkling Air.
  • On the Irish Traditional scene principally on upright bass with major acts including Barry Kerr, Beoga, Liz Carroll, Flook and Grainne Holland.
  • The recordings have been produced by many leading lights on the Irish Scene including Phil Coulter, Michael Keeney, Sean Og Graham, Gareth Dunlop and Pete Wallace and in the US with Jacquire King (Blackbird studios Nashville) and Neilson Hubbard.
  • Check the discography below to listen to the albums from many of these artists.
  • I have been endorsed by MarkBass since 2007
